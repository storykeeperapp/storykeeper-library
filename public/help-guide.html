⚡ Quick Start (5 minutes)

Get started with StoryKeeper in no time!

Step 1: Add Your First Book

You have three ways to add books:

🔍 Search Click "Search Library" or use the search icon. Type a title or author and select from the results.
📷 Scan Click the barcode scanner icon (📷). Point your camera at a book's barcode. It will automatically look up the book and add it to the correct shelf.
✏️ Add Manually Click "Add Book to Library" in the sidebar and enter the details yourself.

Step 2: Organize Your Books

Your books are automatically organized by genre. You can:

  • View by Genre: Click any genre shelf (Fantasy, Mystery, Romance, etc.)
  • See Everything: Go to "Complete Library" to see all your books in one place
  • Mark Favorites: Click the heart icon on any book to add it to your favorites

Step 3: Track Your Reading

Click on any book to open its details, then set your reading status:

  • Want to Read: Books on your To-Be-Read (TBR) list
  • Reading: Books you're currently reading
  • Finished: Books you've completed
💡 Tip: Your reading status is saved to "My Story So Far" where you can see your reading stats and history.

That's It!

You're ready to explore. Click on "My Story So Far" to see your reading statistics, or browse the genre shelves to discover more.

🔗 Platform Import Guides

Import your entire library from your favorite reading platforms:

🌸 Goodreads

  1. Go to Goodreads Export
  2. Scroll down to "Export Library"
  3. Click the Export button
  4. A CSV file will download
  5. In StoryKeeper, click "Platform Connections" in the sidebar
  6. Select "Goodreads" and upload your CSV file
  7. Choose the format (eBook, Audiobook, or Physical)
  8. Review and confirm the import
📝 Note: StoryKeeper automatically detects the format from your Goodreads export (Kindle Edition, Audible Audiobook, Hardcover, etc.) so books go to the correct shelf.

📖 Kindle

  1. Go to Kindle Cloud Reader
  2. Open Developer Console (F12 or Cmd+Option+J)
  3. Go to the Console tab
  4. Copy and paste the export script (provided in StoryKeeper)
  5. Press Enter and a CSV will download
  6. Upload to StoryKeeper via Platform Connections
⏱️ Takes about 2-5 minutes depending on your library size

🎧 Audible

  1. Go to Audible Library
  2. Open Developer Console (F12 or Cmd+Option+J)
  3. Go to the Console tab
  4. Copy and paste the export script (provided in StoryKeeper)
  5. Press Enter and a CSV will download
  6. Upload to StoryKeeper via Platform Connections
✅ Note: Books automatically import as Audiobooks

📚 Other Platforms

Kobo, Apple Books, Chirp, BookFunnel, Google Play, and Nook all have export options. Look for "Export Library" or similar options in your account settings.

Importing Your Reading Status

When you import from Goodreads or Audible, your reading status (finished, currently reading, want to read) is automatically imported too!

✨ Feature Deep-Dives

📚 My TBR Shelf

What is it?

Your personal "To-Be-Read" list. A wishlist of books you want to read but haven't added to your library yet.

How to use it:

  • Click "My TBR Shelf" in the sidebar
  • Search books by title or author
  • Click a result to add it to your TBR
  • When you're ready to read, click "Move to Library" to add it officially

❤️ My Favorites

What is it?

Your personal collection of beloved books. Click the heart icon on any book to add it to favorites.

How to use it:

  • Click any book to open its details
  • Click the heart icon (♥) to add to favorites
  • View all favorites in "My Favorites" from the sidebar

📖 My Story So Far (Book Tracker)

What is it?

Your personal reading dashboard. See how many books you've read, currently reading, and your reading statistics.

What you can see:

  • Books Read: Total finished books (lifetime and this month)
  • Currently Reading: Books you're actively reading
  • Reading Statistics: Monthly and yearly breakdowns
  • Reading Streak: Track your reading consistency

📚 Complete Library

What is it?

View your entire library in one place without genre filtering.

How to use it:

  • Click "Complete Library" in the sidebar
  • See your total book count and breakdown by format
  • Search by title or author to find specific books
  • Click any book to see details

🎯 Genre Shelves

What is it?

Your books organized by category (Fantasy, Mystery, Romance, etc.)

How to use it:

  • Click any genre on the home screen to browse that shelf
  • Search within a genre to find specific books
  • Sort by title, author, or series
  • View counts for eBooks, Audiobooks, and Physical books

🎨 Customize Genre Tiles (Premium Feature)

What is it?

Personalize your genre shelf tiles by uploading custom images and editing genre names.

How to use it:

  • Click "Settings" in the sidebar
  • Select "Customize Genre Tiles"
  • A menu opens showing all your genres
  • Click a genre to edit it
  • Upload a custom image for that genre tile
  • Edit the genre name if desired
  • Save your changes
📝 Note: Custom genre tiles are available based on your subscription tier:
  • Reluctant Reader: Limited to 3 custom tiles
  • Storyteller: Up to 10 custom tiles
  • Librarian: All genres can be customized
  • StoryKeeper: Unlimited customization + advanced options

📷 Barcode Scanner

What is it?

Quickly add physical books by scanning their barcodes.

How to use it:

  • Click the camera icon (📷) in the TBR shelf or import area
  • Point at a book's barcode
  • The book is automatically looked up and added to your Physical Books shelf

👆 Interactive Walkthrough

Follow these steps to explore StoryKeeper hands-on:

1 Go to Home

Open StoryKeeper and look at the main screen. You'll see genre shelves on the left and right sides.

2 Click on a Genre

Click "Fantasy" (or any genre that has books). You'll see all books in that category with a filter and sort options.

3 Click a Book Cover

Click any book to see its details: title, author, description, rating, and current reading status.

4 Change Reading Status

In the book details modal, click a status button (Want to Read, Reading, Finished) to track your progress.

5 Open the Sidebar

Click the menu button (☰) to open the sidebar. You'll see all major features:

  • My Favorites - your loved books
  • Complete Library - all books in one view
  • My TBR Shelf - books to read
  • My Story So Far - reading stats
6 Try the Search

Click "Search Library" and search for a book by title or author. Try adding it to your TBR shelf.

7 Import from a Platform

Click "Platform Connections" to import books from Goodreads, Kindle, Audible, or another platform.

8 Check Your Stats

Click "My Story So Far" to see your reading statistics and track your progress.

🎯 Pro Tip: Try marking a few books as "Finished" and check your stats to see them update in real-time!
