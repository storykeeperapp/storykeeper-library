StoryKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

StoryKeeper ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Book library data — titles, authors, genres, reading status, and cover images that you add to your personal library.
  • User ID — a unique identifier that links your library data to your account for syncing across devices.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To sync your book library across your devices.
  • To provide app features such as genre detection, book search, and reading tracking.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your library data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider, solely to provide the sync feature.

Community Features

StoryKeeper includes an optional community genre voting feature. If you vote on a genre for an author, only the vote (author name + genre) is shared anonymously — never your name, email, or identity.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

StoryKeeper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes within the app. Continued use of StoryKeeper after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
