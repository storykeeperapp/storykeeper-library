Welcome & Thank You

Thank you for being an early tester of StoryKeeper! Your feedback will directly shape the final product. This guide walks you through what the app does, how to test each feature, and how to let us know what you find.

What Is StoryKeeper?

StoryKeeper is a personal library management app that brings all your books — eBooks and audiobooks — from different platforms into one beautiful, cozy home. Think of it as a digital bookshelf where you can:

  • Import books from Audible, Kindle, Kobo, Goodreads, Apple Books, Chirp, and more
  • Browse your library organized by genre, displayed on themed shelves
  • Track what you've read, what you're reading, and what's on your list
  • Customize genre categories with your own names and artwork

Getting Started

Opening the App

Open the link you were provided in any web browser. The app works best in Google Chrome or Safari. You'll land on the home page — a cozy reading nook scene with genre frames arranged along the sides.

📱 Using a phone or tablet? The app fully works on mobile for browsing and uploading CSVs. However, exporting your library from Audible, Kindle, or Chirp requires a desktop computer. If you're on mobile, you can still test by uploading a CSV file you exported earlier on a computer, or skip the import step and focus on the browsing experience.

The Home Screen

The home screen has several elements to explore:

  • Background video — an animated reading nook with ambient sound (click the sound icon to toggle rain and fire crackling)
  • Genre frames — pressed botanical frames along the left and right sides, each representing a book genre. Click one to open that genre's shelf.
  • eBooks / Audiobooks toggle — the button in the top center switches between your eBook library and your audiobook library
  • Hamburger menu (☰) — top left corner. Opens navigation including Platform Connections, Library Settings, and Search
  • Sound toggle — speaker icon in the top area. Turns ambient rain and fire sounds on/off

Testing Checklist

Work through as many of these as you can. Check them off as you go — or just make notes to share with us.

Home Screen

  • The background video loads and plays without issues
  • The sound toggle turns rain/fire sounds on and off cleanly (no popping or delay)
  • The eBooks / Audiobooks toggle button is visible and switches the view
  • The hamburger menu (☰) opens and closes correctly
  • All genre frames are visible and legible on your screen size
  • Hovering over a genre frame highlights it
  • Clicking a genre frame opens that genre's bookshelf
  • The app name and tagline are readable at the top

Genre Frames

  • Right-clicking (or long-pressing on mobile) a genre frame opens a customization menu
  • You can rename a genre using the context menu
  • You can drag and drop the genre frames to reorder them
  • The reordered layout is remembered if you refresh the page
  • The context menu doesn't get cut off at the edges of the screen

Importing Your Library

Go to ☰ Menu → Platform Connections to test imports. See the platform table on the next page for what each one requires.

  • The Platform Connections page opens when selected from the menu
  • The back button works to return to the home screen
  • Each platform tile shows clear instructions for how to export
  • The drag-and-drop CSV upload zone is visible and works (try dragging a file onto it)
  • Clicking the upload zone also opens a file picker
  • After uploading a CSV, a list of detected books appears
  • Each book shows a genre dropdown you can change before importing
  • Clicking Import adds the books to your library
  • Duplicate books are skipped (try importing the same file twice)
  • Imported books appear on the correct genre shelf

Browsing a Genre Shelf

  • Clicking a genre frame opens a scrollable shelf of books in that genre
  • Book covers load and display correctly
  • Tapping/clicking a book cover opens its detail view
  • Book details show title, author, and description
  • You can mark a book as "Reading", "Finished", or "Want to Read"
  • The back button returns you to the home screen
  • The shelf scrolls smoothly on your device

Search

  • The search bar opens from the hamburger menu
  • Typing a title or author returns matching results
  • Clicking a search result opens that book
  • Search works across both eBooks and audiobooks

Library Settings

  • Library Settings opens from the hamburger menu
  • The Re-detect Genres buttons are visible (eBooks / Audiobooks / All Books)
  • Running re-detect shows a progress bar and updates genre assignments

What to Look For

As you test, keep an eye out for the following types of issues:

Bugs

  • Anything that doesn't work as described — buttons that don't respond, pages that don't load, errors that appear
  • Books that import into the wrong genre
  • Data that disappears after refreshing the page
  • Sound that keeps playing after turning it off

Layout & Display Issues

  • Text that is cut off, overlapping, or too small to read
  • Buttons or menus that are off-screen or partially hidden
  • Images that don't load or are distorted
  • Anything that looks broken on your screen size or device

Confusion Points

  • Instructions that are unclear or hard to follow
  • Features you couldn't figure out how to use without help
  • Anything that felt like it was in the wrong place
  • Steps that felt like too many clicks or too complicated

Your Personal Reaction

  • Did the app feel welcoming and cozy, or cold and confusing?
  • Was there anything you wanted to do that you couldn't?
  • What did you genuinely enjoy about the experience?
  • Would you use this to manage your actual book library? Why or why not?

How to Share Your Feedback

📝 When reporting an issue, please include:

  1. What you were trying to do — e.g. "I was trying to import my Audible library"
  2. What happened — e.g. "The page went blank after I clicked upload"
  3. What you expected to happen — e.g. "I expected to see a list of my books"
  4. Your device and browser — e.g. "iPhone 14, Safari" or "Windows laptop, Chrome"
  5. A screenshot if possible — even a phone photo of your screen helps!

Send your feedback to: [your email or feedback link here]

No feedback is too small. If something felt slightly off but you can't quite explain it, tell us anyway — that gut reaction is valuable data.

Known Limitations in This Beta

These are things we already know about and are working on — you don't need to report these:

  • Libby and Hoopla imports are not yet available (coming in a future update)
  • Library data is stored locally in your browser — it will not sync between devices yet
  • Some book covers may not load if the source image is unavailable
  • Genre auto-detection is not perfect — you can always reassign genres manually
